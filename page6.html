INGREDIENTS
1 package puff pastry dough
750 ml fresh milk
100 gr sugar
4 egg yolks
4 1/2 tbsp corn flour
3 tbsp flour
1 tsp vanilla extract
1Â½ tsp rum extract
Piping bags
50 gr sugar
1 egg
Parchment papers

STEPS

For the shell:

Cut the dough into 4 squares and cut each square into 1cm wide strips

Wrap each strip around the cream horn mold, overlapping a little with each round

Beat 1 egg in a bowl and add 2 tbsp of milk. Brush the cream horn with the egg wash and sprinkle with sugar

Place the horn onto a baking tray that has been greased with margarine then bake in 180 degrees Celcius preheated oven for 15-20 minutes until golden brown


For the filling:

heat milk and sugar in boiling pot until the sugar dissolves and the milk reaches simmering point then lower the heat.

In a bowl, combine the corn flour, flour, and egg yolks and mix well. Then add 1 ladleful of the hot milk to the flour mixture. Mix well and strain to remove the lumps.

Add the flour mixture to the hot milk and keep stirring until the milk is thickened. Add vanilla extract and rum extract.

Once the milk has reached the desired consistency, remove from heat and cool it down.

Put the filling in piping bag that has been fitted with piping nozzle, take 1 shell, pierce the side and pipe out the filling into the shell.
