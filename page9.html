INGREDIENTS

500 gr boneless and skinless chicken breast

8 shallots

5 garlic cloves

2 cm fresh ginger

1 cm fresh turmeric

1 tsp coriander seed

3 chilies (cabe keriting)

1 stalk lemongrass

2 tbsp coconut milk powder

1 tsp pepper

2 tbsp oyster sauce

2 tbsp sesame oil

3 tbsp sugar

4 tsp corn flour

2 tbsp light soy sauce

40 large pandan leaves

1 liter cooking oil


STEPS

cut the chicken into cube then prick the chicken with knife/ fork

peel the garlic, shallot, ginger, and turmeric

crush the garlic, ginger and turmeric

break the chilies into smaller pieces

thinly slice the white part of the lemongrass

process the garlic, chilies, shallots, ginger, turmeric, lemongrass, and coriander seeds in food processor until they turn into smooth paste

marinade the chicken with the rest of ingredients except pandan leaves and oil

make a hollow triangle with pandan leaves

place the chicken in the center. Feed the right end through the hole, pull, and tighten.

turn over, feed the other end and secure tightly

if there is an extra space, fill it with another piece of chicken. Secure tightly

repeat the process for the remaining chicken

trim the excess leaves, leaving about 1 inch at each end

deep fry on medium heat until golden brown
