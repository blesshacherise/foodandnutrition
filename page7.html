INGREDIENTS
1 package puff pastry dough
300 gr boneless and skinless chicken breast
1 pc onion
1 clove garlic
1 small can button mushroom
2 tbsp olive oil/ butter
1 pc small green bell pepper
1 pc small yellow/red bell pepper
5 tbsp bbq sauce
150 gr mozzarella cheese/ kraft quick melt
1 egg
salt and black pepper

STEPS

slice the chicken into dice and season with salt and black pepper

slice the onion

cut the bell pepper into julienne

chop the garlic

grate the cheese

beat the egg in small bowl with 2 tbsp of water. Set aside.

Heat the frying pan. Add 2 tbsp of olive oil or butter. Stir fry chopped garlic, and the chicken and cook until the chicken turns golden brown. Add the mushroom

Take half of the puff pastry dough. Put it on cutting board. Arrange the chicken, bell pepper, onion, over the dough, add 5 tbsp of bbq sauce, then top with cheese. roll up the dough into log and brush the top with the beaten egg.

Put it in the oven and bake for 20-25 minutes.
