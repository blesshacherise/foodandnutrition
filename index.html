FOOD & NUTRITION
Andrea(10A), Auryn(10A), Blessha(10B), Jayson(10A), Jessline(10A)

This weekly course is suitable for anyone looking to advance their cooking skills. From an egg to an omelette, every week you learn new and exciting recipes to test your technique and cooking abilities.

MORE ABOUT THE COURSE MORE ABOUT RECIPES
Previous Next × Close
During class, students are given a sheet of paper with steps to make the recipe. Students will then independently create their dish with occasional help from their teacher. Through the recipes given, students learn different cooking techniques such as different styles of chopping and learn how to precisely measure out ingredients. Students are also required to keep the kitchen mess-free after cooking.
