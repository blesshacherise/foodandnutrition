INGREDIENTS

4 eggs                                                        

1 tbsp milk powder

6 egg yolks                                                        

50 gr unsalted margarine + extra for greasing

80 gr sugar                                                         

½ tsp vanilla extract

½ tbsp cake emulsifier (TBM/ Ovalet)                

parchment paper

80 gr flour                                                           

strawberry jam

1 tbsp corn flour



STEPS

melt the margarine

sift the flour, corn flour, and milk powder

Using electric hand mixer, beat eggs, sugar, and cake emulsifier until the mixture become white and double in size. (increase the speed every 1 minute)

fold in the flour mixture gradually. Mix well

add the melted margarine gradually. Mix well

add the vanilla extract. Mix well

grease the baking tray with margarine and line with baking paper

pour the cake batter into the tray and bake in 200 C preheated oven for about 10-15 minutes

take out the cake from oven and put it up side down on another baking tray/ cutting board that has been lined with baking paper

remove the baking paper and spread the strawberry jam on the cake

roll the cake up
